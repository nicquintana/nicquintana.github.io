Baseball: UMD vs. UMBC

Maryland baseball got back in the win column Tuesday afternoon with a 12-6 win over UMBC. The Terps plated six runs in the 8th inning, propelling them to the midweek victory.

Field Hockey: UMD vs. Georgetown

In an absolute beatdown, No. 2 Maryland field hockey defeated Georgetown Tuesday afternoon, 11-0. Riley Donnelly and Bibi Donraadt each recorded hat tricks, and the Terps have now won six games in a row.

Field Hockey: UMD vs. Indiana

No. 2 Maryland field hockey moved to 12-1 on the season Friday night, taking down Indiana, 5-1. Four of Maryland's goals came during the first half, when the Terps out-shot the Hoosiers 21-1.

Gymnastics: UMD vs. Minnesota

Despite earning its second-best score of the season on the beam and having several gymnasts earn meet-high scores, Maryland gymnastics couldn't keep up with a well-rounded Minnesota squad, falling 196.650 to 196.800.
